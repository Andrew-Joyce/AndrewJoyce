    Overview

    My project idea is to create a central web portal/app that people can use after being tested and diagnosed with Covid-19 to apply for financial, well-being and care assistance.

    The purpose of this application is to provide people a central contact point to access the support they will need to quarantine for the 14-day mandatory period. The service should provide an individual and their families a seamless experience in applying for the difference assistance they may need throughout this period.

    Motivation

    For people tested and diagnosed with Covid-19, it causes an immediate disruption to their lives. People who test positive are required to self-isolate for 14 days to minimise the possible spread of the virus in the community. They are no longer able to work or have access to basic necessities needed to support themselves through this period.

    Both the Federal and State governments have put in place support packages to assist people through this period. The processes to access these services are disparate for the average person. They can be streamlined and simplified to reduce the stress of the situation.

    Description

    Outlined below is the process that a person would follow to access Federal and State assistance following taking a Covid-19 test.

    1. Test for Covid-19

    As part of taking a Covid-19 test, a patient provides their contact details for their results. The details include their name, address, email, and phone number.

    Once completed, the patient is handed a unique identifier from the medical officer who administered the test and information material relating to the 14-day quarantine period.

    The information material will reference the new web portal/app to register for Federal and State government assistance.

    2. Request Support

    Following receiving the information, the patient can register online via a website or mobile app for assistance.

    The following information is to be captured as part of the registration process:

    The unique identifier provided by the medical officer

  • Name
  • Address
  • Email: and,
  • Government ID (license or passport number)
  • The details will be verified to confirm the identity.

    • Following confirmation of their identity, the site will list out the assistance they can receive before or after their diagnosis is confirmed.

    If the patient is eligible for any financial assistance, they will nominate the bank account details to receive their payment.

    3. Diagnosis Confirmed

    For those patients who have registered for support, their requests will be electronically directed to the appropriate government agencies once a diagnosis is confirmed.

    If the diagnosis is negative, there is no further action and their details will be deleted within 24 hours.

    4. Receive Support

    Text and email notifications will be sent to the patient upon their requests for support being registered with all the appropriate government agencies.

    Text Message

    The text message will provide a brief summary to confirm that their requests have been actioned. It will also confirm that further information relating to the assistance they will receive will be provided by email.

    Email

    The email confirmation will provide the following information in detail:

    1. Financial Support

    An itemised summary of the payments they are entitled to receive, the agencies who will make the payments, the amount of each payment, and the date they will be paid.

    2. Medical Certificate

    A PDF medical certificate that the patient can supply to their workplace or education service to excuse them from attendance

    3. Health and Support services

    A summary of support services available to the patient throughout the quarantine period to help manage their well-being

    4. Necessities package

    A link to a distribution supplier, where a patient can order the necessities (bread, milk, toilet paper) they may require immediately to support themselves while quarantined.
